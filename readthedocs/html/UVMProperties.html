uvm.user_coverage

(uvm_user_coverage) 

Specifies all the identifiers globally at the top level of the register specification in the block description. This will generate an enum of type “uvm_reg_cvr_t”

<IDENTIFIER_NAME1>:<its_value>,

<IDENTIFIER_NAME2>:<its_value>


(Values of the coverage identifiers should be one-hot values. This is to avoid collisions with pre-defined UVM, vendor-defined, and user-defined coverage modelidentifiers, bits 0 through 7 are reserved for UVM, bits 8 through 15 are reserved for vendors, and bits 16through 23 are reserved for users.)

none

Chip, 

Block,
