rtl.hw_latch=high


The reg_BLH is latch high register. Property {rtl.hw_latch=high} latches the high data from the HW side. {rtl.sw_read=clear} will clear the register, whenever there is read transaction from the SW side.

rtl.hw_latch=low


This reg_BLL is latch low register. Property {rtl.hw_latch=low}  will latch the low data from the HW side.
